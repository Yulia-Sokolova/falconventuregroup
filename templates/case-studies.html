SportsTalk 24/7

Designing the user experience for a real-time fan engagement platform with AI-powered moderation.

The Challenge

SportsTalk 24/7 needed to bring five distinct engagement tools — live chat, private messaging, comments, emotion reactions, and polls — into a single, intuitive platform for sports fans. The core UX challenge: how do you moderate conversations at scale during live events with unlimited participants, without killing the energy of real-time fan interaction?

Our Approach

We mapped every user journey — from a first-time fan dropping into a live game chat to a moderator reviewing flagged content during a broadcast. We designed the moderation flow to combine AI filtering with community-driven reporting, so toxic content gets caught without bottlenecking the conversation. The result: a platform where moderation feels invisible to good-faith users.

Mobile-first comments with threaded replies and democratic sorting

Polls designed for instant fan engagement and data collection

What We Delivered

  • Interface design for five engagement tools — live chat, messenger, comments, reactions, and polls — unified under a consistent, intuitive design language
  • Moderation UX that layers AI filtering with community reporting, giving moderators clear workflows without disrupting the fan experience
  • Brand voice guidelines — professional yet approachable tone captured in the tagline "Copy - Paste - Go!" that makes a complex platform feel simple
  • Mobile-first responsive design ensuring every feature works seamlessly from stadium seats to living room couches

"Falcon Venture Group understood our challenge immediately — we needed to make real-time moderation feel effortless for both our team and our users. They designed flows that handle the complexity behind the scenes while keeping the fan experience fast and fun. The platform feels simple, and that's the hardest thing to achieve."

— Product Lead, SportsTalk 24/7

The Challenge

SPLASH Fashion TV covers the world's most prestigious fashion events — New York Fashion Week, Milan Fashion Week, London Fashion Week — and needed a digital presence that matched the caliber of the designers they feature. The platform had to feel as polished as a runway show while serving a diverse global audience across cultures and devices.

Our Approach

We started with the brand's philosophy — "Simply Beautiful" — and let it drive every design decision. The UX strips away excess to put photography and editorial content at the center. We designed the information architecture to serve multiple content verticals (runway, street style, lifestyle) without overwhelming the viewer, and ensured the experience translates seamlessly for a global fashion audience.

What We Delivered

  • Brand voice definition — established the "Simply Beautiful" editorial tone that carries across all content, from headlines to photo captions, reflecting minimalist sophistication
  • Information architecture designed to serve News, Live coverage, and Paraiso lifestyle verticals without fragmenting the audience
  • Visual design system — clean, spacious grid layouts that let high-fashion photography breathe, with elegant typography and a neutral palette that never competes with the content
  • Global audience UX — navigation and content structure optimized for international visitors covering fashion weeks across New York, Milan, and London

"We needed our digital presence to feel as elevated as the designers we cover. Falcon Venture Group gave us a brand voice and a user experience that captures the essence of what we do — 'Simply Beautiful' isn't just a tagline, it's how every page feels. Our global audience navigates the site effortlessly whether they're following NYFW from New York or Milan Fashion Week from Tokyo."

— Creative Director, SPLASH Fashion TV
