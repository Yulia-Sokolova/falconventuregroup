# Privacy Policy

*Last updated: March 10, 2026*

Falcon Venture Group ("we," "us," or "our") operates the website at falconventuregroup.com. This policy describes how we handle information in connection with our website.

## Information We Collect

We do not collect any data about visitors to our website. We do not use cookies, tracking pixels, analytics services, or any other form of visitor monitoring.

The only information we receive is what you voluntarily provide through our contact form:

- Your name
- Your email address
- The contents of your message

## How We Use Your Information

We use contact form submissions solely to respond to your inquiry. That's it.

## Information Sharing

We do not share, sell, rent, or disclose your information to any third parties for any purpose.

## Cookies and Tracking

We do not use cookies. We do not use any analytics or tracking tools. We do not serve ads or use advertising pixels.

## Data Retention

Contact form submissions are retained only as long as necessary to respond to your inquiry and maintain a record of our correspondence.

## Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this policy, we will update the date at the top of this page.

## Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please [contact us](/contact).
